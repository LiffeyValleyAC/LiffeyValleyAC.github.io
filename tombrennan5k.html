TOM BRENNAN 2026

Kick off your 2026 in fine style in the Phoenix Park as you run or jog the Tom Brennan Memorial 5k race. With our usual professionalism and trademark warmth and welcome, Liffey Valley AC is delighted to welcome you back to this flat, fast, pb-friendly course. One of the Phoenix Park's longest running events, we guarantee you an enjoyable morning, (9.30 am start) and the ideal beginning to your new year.

FAQ

Q
Are race entries open?
A
Yes. You can register in eventmaster.ie. Online ticket sales will be available between September 01, 2025 at 09:00 and December 30, 2025 at 20:00.
Q
Can I transfer my entry?
A
Yes. You can transfer your entry and number to another person at the MyRunResults Participants List page by following the instructions here.
NOTE: If you had your number posted and wish to transfer, you must exchange that number to the new participant. NO NEW NUMBER WILL BE ISSUED ON THE DAY.
Q
Can I register on the morning of the race?
A
Unfortunately, this is not possible. Race entries must be registered via the Event Master platform. Entries close on December 30th or earlier, if the race sells out before this date.
Q
Where is the START LINE?
A
The race will start on the Furze Road, which is just off Chesterfield Avenue in the Phoenix Park.
The start line is 500 meters from the Number Collection area on the Furze Road / Chesterfield Avenue Junction.
Q
What is the START TIME?
A
The race starts at 9:30 AM.
Q
If I missed a postage entry, where do I collect my number?
A
The Number Collection area is on the Furze Road / Chesterfield Avenue Junction. Please quote your number at the collection desk to speed the process up and allow help to avoid queues. You will receive a text message to the mobile number provided with your bib number.
Q
What time can I collect my number?
A
Number Collection will be open from 8:00 AM.
Q
Where can I park my car?
A
Please visit Phoenix Park Opening Hours for details on car parking.
NOTE: Knockmaroon car park, inside Knockmaroon Gate will be CLOSED.
Q
Where do I collect my race gift?
A
Please collect your bobble hat AFTER you have crossed the finish line and completed the race.
Q
Will there be water?
A
Yes, water will be available approximately 20 meters after the finish line.
Q
Will there be toilets?
A
Yes, there will be portaloos available on Furze Road. There are also toilets at the Papal Cross Car Park.
Q
Will there be refreshments?
A
Last Lap Cafe will have hot drinks & refreshments to purchase.
Q
Will there be prizes presented?
A

Yes. All prizes will be presented on the day and will be awarded as follows:

  • First three men and women
  • Bonus prize €200 for first man/first woman if the course record is broken
  • First three men’s and women’s teams (3 to score)
  • First veteran men and women +35, +40, +45, +50, +55, +60, +65, +70, +75
  • 3+ entrants per age group required for prize award
  • First junior man and woman
