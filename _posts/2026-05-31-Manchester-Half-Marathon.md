<p><strong>Congratulations to Joseph Whittle</strong> on an excellent performance at the <strong>Great Manchester Run Half Marathon</strong> on Sunday.</p>

<p>Joe completed the challenging 13.1 mile course in an impressive time of <strong>1:28:55 hr</strong>, taking in some of Manchester’s most iconic landmarks, including <strong>Old Trafford</strong> and <strong>Salford Quays</strong>, before crossing the finish line on <strong>Deansgate</strong>.</p>

<p>A fantastic achievement and another great result representing the club. <strong>Well done, Joe!</strong> 🔵🟡</p>
