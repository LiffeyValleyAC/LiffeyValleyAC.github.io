<h4>Irish Runner 5K / National 5K Championships - Phoenix Park</h4>

<p><strong>Well done</strong> to everyone who raced the <strong>Irish Runner 5K Challenge 2026</strong> in Phoenix Park on Saturday, 16 May 2026. The event also incorporated the <strong>National 5K Championships</strong> and had a huge field, with <strong>2,005 finishers</strong> listed by MyRunResults.</p>

<p>Liffey Valley AC had <strong>21 finishers</strong> on the day, with Geoffrey Alexandre leading the club home in <strong>16:00</strong> and strong team performances across the national championship categories.</p>
