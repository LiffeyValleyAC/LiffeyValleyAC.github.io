---
title: Great results at the National Intermediate and Master XC Championships
location: Abbostwon, Co. Dublin
---

  Well done to <strong>all our athletes</strong> who competed at the
  <strong>National Intermediate &amp; Masters Cross Country Championships</strong>
  in Abbostwon, Co. Dublin today.
  
  This was the last race of the Cross Country season.

<p>The race was held on a very muddy <strong>1km loop circuit</strong>. The distances for each category were as follows: <br>
<strong>Inter Men 6km</strong> (6 laps)<br>
<strong>Inter Women 4km</strong> (4 laps)<br>
<strong>Masters Men 6km</strong> (6 laps)<br>
<strong>Masters Women 3km</strong> (3 laps)<br>
<strong>Masters +65 Men 3km</strong> (3 laps).</p>
