location: Phoenix Park, Co. Dublin.
---

Liffey Valley AC athletes delivered outstanding performances at the **Great Ireland Run 10km** in Phoenix Park on Sunday, competing against a quality field of **3,202 finishers** in challenging cold and windy conditions.

### Top Individual Performances

**Pierce Geoghegan** led the club home in brilliant fashion, securing **13th place overall** with an impressive time of **32:16**, earning him **1st place in the M40 category**.

**Mark Naylor** continued his excellent form with **19th place** in **32:40**, while **David Carroll** (24th, 33:18) and **Geoffrey Alexandre** (27th, 33:33) packed well inside the top with 2nd and 3rd place finishes in the competitive M35 category.

On the women's side, **Elle O'Driscoll** was the club's top performer with **37:12** for **84th place overall**, followed by **Mollie O'Donnell** in **37:59** (106th) winning 1st place in the W35 category. 

Other notable finishes included **Simon Monds** (37:40, M45), **Jason McGlone** (38:42, MS), **Neil Duffy** (39:24, M45), and **Cliodhna Tutty Bardon** (40:07, FS).

A total of **18 LVAC athletes** toed the line, with PBs achieved and strong showings across all age categories. Well done to everyone who raced! 🟡🔵 

### Team Results

The team competition was fierce, with Liffey Valley AC finishing in:<br>
**1st place Team Senior Women**<br>
**2nd place Team Senior Men**
