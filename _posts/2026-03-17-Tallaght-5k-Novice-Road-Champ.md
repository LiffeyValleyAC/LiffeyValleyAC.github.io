---
title: Dublin 5k Novice Road Championships
location: Tallaght, Co. Dublin.
---
<p>
  <strong>Well done to all our athletes</strong> who took part in the
  <strong>Dublin 5k Novice Road Championships</strong> in Tallaght, Co. Dublin
  on Sunday 17th March 2026 🏃‍♂️🏃‍♀️, St. Patrick’s Day.<br>
  We had an excellent turnout with <strong>29 Liffey Valley A.C. runners</strong>
  competing in a very strong field of <strong>660 athletes</strong> 👏
</p>

<p>
  Leading the way for the club were our <strong>Top 3 Men</strong> 💪:<br>
  <strong>Geoffrey Alexandre (17th - 16:18)</strong><br>
  <strong>Richard Finegan (43rd - 17:26)</strong><br>
  <strong>Jason Mc Glone (84th - 18:31)</strong><br><br>
  On the women’s side, our <strong>Top 3 Women</strong> 🌟 were<br>
  <strong>Siobhan Nugent (209th - 20:47)</strong><br>
  <strong>Dearbhaile Rooney (210th - 20:47)</strong><br>
  <strong>Nikki Lynch (232nd - 21:05)</strong><br>
</p>

<p>
  There were also fantastic <strong>age category performances</strong> 👏:<br>
  <strong>Eddie McGrath</strong> secured <strong>3rd place in M55</strong> 🥉<br>
  <strong>Margaret O’Friel</strong> took <strong>3rd place in F60</strong> 🥉<br>
  <strong>Pat Collins</strong> led the way with an outstanding
  <strong>1st place in M70</strong> 🥇, followed by<br>
  <strong>Joe Cooper</strong> in <strong>3rd place</strong> 🥉.
</p>

<p>
  At the top of the field, the race winners in the men’s were<br>
  <strong>Colm Rooney (15:07)</strong> 🥇<br>
  <strong>Ben Guiden (15:15)</strong> 🥈<br>
  <strong>Mahad Mohamed Egaal (15:17)</strong> 🥉<br>

  In the women’s race<br>
  <strong>Maebh Brannigan (16:48)</strong> 🥇<br>
  <strong>Lisa Madden (17:12)</strong> 🥈<br>
  <strong>Helena Butterly (17:21)</strong> 🥉<br>
</p>

<p>
  <strong>Great racing all round</strong>, with strong performances, team spirit
  and plenty to build on for the rest of the season. Congratulations to everyone
  who represented the club so well! 💙💛
</p>
