<h4>🥇 Team Gold - 5km Road Race M65</h4>

<p>Huge congratulations to <strong>Tom Brennan</strong> who finished <strong>8th</strong> in his category at the European Masters 5k Road Championships, with a time of 19:24 min! 🙌</p>

<p>But the biggest celebration goes to the <strong>Irish M65 team</strong> who claimed <strong>TEAM GOLD</strong> 🥇 with Tom contributing to this fantastic achievement. The team placed 3rd, 7th and 8th - an outstanding performance on the international stage!</p>

<h4>🏃‍♂️ 800m M55 - Round 1 Heat 4</h4>

<p>Well done to <strong>Donal Iremonger</strong> who placed <strong>4th</strong> in his 800m heat at the European Masters Indoor Championships with a time of <strong>2:15.15 min</strong>,just missing out on qualification for the final. A tremendous effort! 👏👏👏</p>
