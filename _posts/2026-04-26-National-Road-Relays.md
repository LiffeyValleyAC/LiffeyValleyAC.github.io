---
title: National Road Relay Championships
location: Raheny, Dublin.
---

We had five teams competing at the National Road Relays on April 26th in Raheny, Dublin, one of the premier club events on the Irish athletics calendar.

💙 Senior Men - 11th<br>
💛 Senior Women (Team A) - 10th<br>
💛 Senior Women (Team B) - 28th<br>
💙 Master Men 35+ (Team A) - 3rd <b>Bronze 🥉</b><br>
💙 Master Men 35+ (Team B) - 17th<br>

A fantastic performance from our Master Men 35+ Team A, securing a podium finish in a highly competitive field.

The event featured clubs from across the country competing for national honours over a challenging relay format on the looped course in Raheny.

Thanks to Raheny Shamrock AC for hosting another fantastic day of racing, as always.
