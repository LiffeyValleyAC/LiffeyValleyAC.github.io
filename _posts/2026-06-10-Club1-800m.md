---
title: LVAC C1 of 3 - 800m Track Championships
location: Tallaght, Dublin
---

<p>Our <strong>LVAC Club 1 of 3 - 800m Track Championships</strong> took place at <strong>Tallaght Track</strong> on <strong>18th June 2025</strong>, bringing together 26 athletes of all levels for an exciting evening of middle-distance racing.</p>

<p>Congratulations to all athletes who took part, from those racing the track for the first time to our experienced competitors battling for club medals and personal bests. The competitive spirit was on full display, with strong performances across both the men's and women's races.</p>

<p>A special congratulations to our medal winners: <strong>Ellen Cronin</strong>, <strong>Nikki Lynch</strong> and <strong>Dearbhaile Rooney</strong> in the Women's Championship, and <strong>Geoffrey Alexandre</strong>, <strong>Richard Finegan</strong> and <strong>Michael O'Connor</strong> in the Men's Championship.</p>

<p>Thank you to all athletes, coaches, volunteers and officials who helped make the event such a success.</p>
