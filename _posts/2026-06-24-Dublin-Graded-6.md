A huge thank you to all <b>Liffey Valley Athletic Club</b> athletes who participated in the sixth Graded Meeting of the 2026 Track Season at <b>Morton Stadium</b> on <b>June 24th 2026</b>. Your dedication and hard work truly shone through as you hit the track and gave it your all in the <b>1500m</b> distance.<br>

A special mention goes to <b>David Carroll</b> and <b>Geoffrey Alexandre</b> for their <b>PBs</b>, with strong runs also from <b>Orla Gordon</b>, <b>Richard Finegan</b>, <b>James Foley</b>, <b>Simon Monds</b>, <b>Bernard O'Sullivan</b>, <b>Patrick Doyle</b>, and <b>Neil Duffy</b>. These excellent results are another clear example of the depth and consistency within <b>Liffey Valley AC</b> and the support provided by <b>Jim Davis</b> and <b>Eddie McGrath</b>.<br>

Well done to everyone who represented the club on the night. 🔵🟡
