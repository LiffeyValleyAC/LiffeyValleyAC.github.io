title: Dublin Graded Meeting 5 of 8
location: DSD, Dublin
---

A huge thank you to all <b>Liffey Valley Athletic Club</b> athletes who participated in the fifth Graded Meeting of the 2026 Track Season at <b>DSD</b> on <b>June 13th 2026</b>. Your dedication and hard work truly shone through as you hit the track and gave it your all in the <b>3000m</b> distance.<br>

A special mention goes to the outstanding performances where <b>Orla Gordon claimed 1st place</b> in the Women's D race and <b>Neil Duffy won the Men's D race</b>. These excellent results are another clear example of the strength of the club’s distance running programme and show how the coaching strategy led by <b>Jim Davis</b>, together with <b>Eddie McGrath</b>, continues to deliver remarkable results for <b>Liffey Valley AC</b>.<br>

Well done to Orla, Neil, and everyone who represented the club on the night. 🔵🟡
