---
title: Strong Performances at the National Novice XC
location: Abbottstown, Co. Dublin
---

The National Novice Cross Country Championships took place in Abbotstown, Dublin, on Sunday, December 7, 2025. The novice men's 6000m title was won by Callum Barron of Kilkenny City Harriers AC.

Well done to the LVAC Men and Woman Teams who raced in a very competive National Novice Cross Country Championship, in challenging conditions.<br>

19th Men Team <br>
19th Women Team <br>
<b>Ennis Track A.C</b> was the winner in both categories<br>
