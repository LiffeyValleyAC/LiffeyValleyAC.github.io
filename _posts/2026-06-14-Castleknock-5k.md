<p><strong>Well done</strong> to our <strong>Liffey Valley AC</strong> athletes who took part in <strong>The 2026 Castleknock 5km</strong> on <strong>Sunday 14th June</strong>.</p>

<p>Congratulations to <strong>Ruth Murphy</strong>, <strong>Pat Collins</strong>, <strong>Clare Kavanagh</strong>, <strong>Joe Cooper</strong>, <strong>Paddy Reilly</strong>, and <strong>Stephen Coughlan</strong> on representing the club at this wonderful local community event.</p>

<p>A great morning of running, with profits from the event supporting <strong>St. Francis Hospice</strong> and <strong>Metro Saint Brigids Athletic Club</strong>.</p>
