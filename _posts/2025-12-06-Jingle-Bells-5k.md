---
title: Jingle Bells 5k 2025 with new personal bests
location: Phoenix Park, Dublin
---

On a cool and cloudy morning, the 2025 <b>Jingle Bells 5k Run</b> took place under temperatures of 10°C / 8°C, with passing clouds, 94% humidity, a 985 mbar barometer, and a WSW wind at 14 km/h. <b>1914</b> participants finished the race. Despite the challenging weather, the <b>Liffey Valley A.C.</b> athletes delivered outstanding performances, with most runners achieving new personal bests. The team showed exceptional depth and determination, led by <b>Pierce Geoghegan’s</b> remarkable <b>15:14</b> finish (2nd in M40 category), closely followed by strong sub-16 efforts from <b>Geoffrey Alexandre</b> and <b>Mark Naylor</b>. Excellent runs across all categories, including superb times from <b>Tom O’Connor</b> (1st in M60 category), <b>Neil Duffy</b>, <b>Luke Ryder</b>, <b>Bernard O’Sullivan</b>, <b>Martin Dorgan</b>, and <b>Pat Collins</b>, highlighted a fantastic day for the club and a great start to the festive racing season.
