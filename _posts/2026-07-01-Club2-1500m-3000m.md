---
title: LVAC Club 2 of 3 - 1500m W and 3000m M Track Championships
location: Tallaght Track, Dublin
---

<p>Our <strong>LVAC Club 2 of 3 - 1500m/3000m Track Championships</strong> took place at <strong>Tallaght Track</strong> on <strong>1st July 2026</strong>, bringing together 22 athletes for a competitive evening of middle-distance racing.</p>

<p>Congratulations to everyone who raced, from first-time track athletes to seasoned club competitors. The night produced excellent performances across both events, with strong racing throughout the women's 1500m and men's 3000m.</p>

<p>A special congratulations to our medal winners: <strong>Dearbhaile Rooney</strong>, <strong>Maeve Gilmartin</strong> and <strong>Orla Gordon</strong> in the Women's Championship, and <strong>David Carroll</strong>, <strong>Pierce Geoghegan</strong> and <strong>Mark Naylor</strong> in the Men's Championship.</p>

<p>Thank you to all athletes, coaches, volunteers and officials who helped make the evening such a success.</p>

See you at the next club race! 🏃‍♂️🏃‍♀️

C3 of 3 – Guess Your Time!
📅 Wednesday, 8 July 2026, 📍 Tallaght, 🕖 7:15 pm, 📏 1 Mile

Leave your watch behind! This fun event is <strong>run without watches or GPS devices</strong>. Before the start, you'll predict your finishing time, and the runners who finish closest to their predicted time will win cash prizes. Good luck, and we hope to see you all there! 💰🎯
