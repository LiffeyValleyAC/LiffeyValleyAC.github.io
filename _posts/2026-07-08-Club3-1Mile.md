<p>⏱️ <strong>Guess Your Time - 1 Mile Event</strong> 🏃‍♀️🏃‍♂️</p>

<p>Our latest club race took place on <strong>Wednesday 8 July 2026</strong> in <strong>Tallaght</strong>, with athletes taking on the <strong>Guess Your Time</strong> challenge.</p>

<p>The aim was to predict your finishing time before the race and see how close the prediction was to your finishing time.</p>

<p>Well done to everyone who took part. Congratulations to our prize winners - <strong>David Carroll</strong> and <strong>Nikki Lynch</strong> - who finished exactly on their predicted time, and <strong>Richard Finegan</strong> - just 1 second away from his predicted time.</p>

<p>Thanks to everyone who came along and helped make it a great club evening.</p>
