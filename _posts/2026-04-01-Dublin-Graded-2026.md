<b>Liffey Valley Athletic Club</b> athletes, the <b>2026 track season</b> is almost here and it’s time to bring your winter training to the track! This year’s <b>Dublin Graded Leagues</b> will feature <b>8 fully confirmed Graded Meetings</b>, taking place between <b>May and July 2026</b> across <b>four top-class track venues</b> in Dublin.

The series will be held at <b>Morton Stadium (Santry)</b>, <b>Irishtown Stadium</b>, <b>DSD Campus (Rathfarnham)</b>, and <b>Lucan Harriers Track</b>, offering a mix of weekend and midweek racing to suit all athletes.

The 2026 programme includes a wide range of <b>track events</b> such as <b>100m, 200m, 400m, 800m, 1500m, 3000m, 5000m and 10000m</b>, along with a full programme of <b>field events</b>. All races are graded <b>A to D</b>, ensuring competitive racing for athletes of all standards.

📌 <b>What’s new and important for 2026:</b>
<ul>
  <li>✔️ The <b>mile has been replaced by the 1500m</b> across the series</li>
  <li>✔️ <b>Dublin Senior Championships</b> will be incorporated into Meetings <b>1, 2, 3 and 4</b></li>
  <li>✔️ <b>Electronic timing</b> at all meetings</li>
  <li>✔️ <b>Live streaming</b> planned for all eight meetings</li>
  <li>✔️ A full <b>Grand Prix series</b> for both track and field events</li>
</ul>

This is the perfect opportunity to race on fast tracks, gain championship experience, and chase <b>personal bests</b> throughout the summer. Whether you’re targeting podium finishes, consistent graded racing, or simply sharpening your race skills, the <b>2026 Dublin Graded Leagues</b> are a key part of the season calendar.

<b>Entries are made online via Athletics Ireland</b>, with entries opening approximately 10 days before each meeting and closing around 5 days beforehand. No entries on the day.

In addition to the Dublin Graded Leagues, the <b>Club Track Championship</b> for the 2026 season is officially composed of three key races: <b>C1, C2, and C3</b>. All athletes, as always, are asked to support the championship race by participating and giving their best effort.

Let’s make <b>Track Season 2026</b> a strong one for <b>LVAC</b> 💪🔥
