---
title: LVAC XC Championships
location: Dog's Pond, Phoenix Park, Dublin
---
<p>
<strong>Well done to all who attended</strong> on a truly <strong>special and emotional day for the club</strong>, as our <strong>Club XC Championships</strong> took place in the <strong>Phoenix Park</strong> on a breezy morning with welcome sunshine during the race, marking the <strong>4th of 6 Winter League races</strong>.  
</p>

<p>On the course, <strong>Women and M60+</strong> completed <strong>5km (2 laps)</strong> around the Dog Pond, while the <strong>Men</strong> raced <strong>10km (4 laps)</strong>.
</p>
<p>A huge congratulations to our <strong>XC Champions</strong>, <strong>Pierce Geoghegan</strong> and <strong>Siobhán Nugent</strong>, superbly supported by <strong>Mark Naylor, Simon Monds, Lynn Devitt and Orla Gordon</strong>. 
</p>
<p>The day was made even more meaningful by the presence of so many friends of the club, from current members to familiar faces from years gone by, coming together in a <strong>touching tribute to everything Nick represented</strong>. It was our privilege to welcome <strong>Kathryn and Colm, Jean, Jimmy, Mary and Suzanne</strong> to the post-race presentations. 
</p>
<p>We were also delighted to crown <strong>Aoife Brady</strong> as the <strong>first-ever winner of the Nick Davis Perpetual Cup</strong>, a <strong>new club recognition introduced in memory of Nick Davis</strong>, honouring his legacy, commitment and outstanding performance, with Aoife proudly becoming <strong>the first name engraved on the cup</strong>.
</p>
<p>Congratulations to all our athletes and, as always, a <strong>big thank you to our officials</strong> for making the race possible.
</p>
