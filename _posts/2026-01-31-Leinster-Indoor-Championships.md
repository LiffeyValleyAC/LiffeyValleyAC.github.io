Our athletes delivered <strong>outstanding performances</strong> at the
  <strong>Leinster Indoor Track Championships</strong>, held at the
  <strong>fabulous Athlone International Arena</strong>.
  With multiple gold medals and strong championship runs across the 1500 m and
  800 m events, the club was proudly represented on the track, showcasing
  <strong>talent, experience, and competitive spirit</strong> at every level.
