---
title: Dublin Graded Meeting 7 of 8
location: Lucan Track, Dublin
---

A huge thank you to all <b>Liffey Valley Athletic Club</b> athletes who competed at the seventh Graded Meeting of the 2026 Track Season at <b>Lucan Track</b> on <b>Wednesday 15/07/26</b>. The club was represented across the <b>800m</b> and <b>3000m</b> events, with some excellent runs on the night.<br>

A special mention goes to <b>Richard Finegan</b> and <b>James Foley</b> for their <b>PBs</b>, with strong runs also from <b>Orla Gordon</b>, <b>Katie Barry</b>, <b>Geoffrey Alexandre</b>, <b>David Carroll</b>, and <b>Patrick Doyle</b>. These results are another clear example of the depth and consistency within <b>Liffey Valley AC</b> and the support provided by <b>Jim Davis</b> and <b>Eddie McGrath</b>.<br>

Well done to everyone who represented the club on the night.
