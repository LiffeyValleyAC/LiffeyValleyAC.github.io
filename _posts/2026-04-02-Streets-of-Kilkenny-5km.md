title: Streets of Kilkenny 5km
location: Kilkenny, Ireland
---

The annual **Streets of Kilkenny 5k** is a popular race held on Holy Thursday evening, **2nd April**. Taking in a two-lap circuit around the medieval city, it is a fast flat course with a carnival atmosphere. Street closures and supporter areas with live music added to the occasion. A quality field of **924** runners competed.

Well done to Elle, Mollie and Michael who represented Liffey Valley AC 🟡🔵

Strong racing from the trio, with **Mollie O'Donnell** finishing in **17:55** for 128th place overall and 17th among 435 women (17th of 146 in the W35 category). **Elle O'Driscoll** crossed the line in **18:03** for 134th place overall and 18th among 435 women (18th of 146 in the W35 category). Both athletes finished inside the top 20 women in a quality field 💪 👏
