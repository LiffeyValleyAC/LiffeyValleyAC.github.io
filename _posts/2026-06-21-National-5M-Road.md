title: Biofreeze Irish Runner 5 Mile Road Championships.
location: Phoenix Park, Dublin
---

<p><strong>Thank you and congratulations to everyone who took part in the Irish Runner 5 Mile in Phoenix Park on Sunday, June 21st, 2026.</strong></p>

<p>It was a clear and pleasant summer morning, providing the perfect setting for the second race of the 2026 Athletics Ireland Race Series. Liffey Valley AC athletes once again delivered a series of excellent performances, with both the men's and women's teams securing an outstanding <strong>5th place in the Championship standings</strong>.</p>

<p>A special congratulations goes to <strong>Geoffrey Alexandre</strong>, who claimed the <strong>silver medal in the M35 Championship category</strong>. This marks his <strong>fifth Championship medal since April</strong>, an exceptional achievement that highlights his remarkable consistency throughout the season.</p>

<p>Congratulations also to <strong>Path</strong>, who earned the <strong>bronze medal in his Championship category</strong>, adding another podium finish for the club.</p>

<p>Well done to every Liffey Valley AC athlete who represented the club. Your commitment, teamwork, and determination continue to make the club proud. 🔵🟡</p>
