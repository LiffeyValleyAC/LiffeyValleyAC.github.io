LVAC 50th Anniversary - September 21st 2024

Celebrating 50 Years of Community and Achievement

On September 24th, 1974, our journey began. And today, we proudly celebrate five decades of friendship, dedication, and success. From lifelong bonds to remarkable personal and team milestones, the impact of our club on the lives of so many has been truly profound.

We kicked off the anniversary weekend in style with an inspiring morning of competition at the Myles Cullen and George Harrison races in Phoenix Park, a true showcase of our athletes' talent and team spirit.

An Unforgettable Evening of Celebration

Saturday night was nothing short of magical as we came together at the Guinness Storehouse to celebrate our 50th anniversary. It was a night to reflect on our incredible journey and toast to the future with joy and laughter.

A heartfelt thank you to Hilary and the entire organising committee for making the evening so memorable. Special thanks to Brigid, Annie, and everyone who contributed to this wonderful celebration.

Here's to many more years of running, friendship, and shared success!

Members of LVAC enjoying the celebration together.

A Gathering for the History Books

Club members gathered to celebrate five decades of passion for athletics. Camaraderie and sporting spirit were evident as both long-standing and new members shared anecdotes and memories that have shaped the club's identity throughout the years.

Jim Davis talking to the attendees.

Half a Century of Achievements

During the evening, tribute was paid to the club's founders and those who have significantly contributed to its development. From its humble beginnings to becoming one of the most respected clubs in the region, LVAC has proven that with dedication and passion, all obstacles can be overcome.

The Anniversary Cake: A beautifully decorated cake to mark the occasion.

Sweet Celebration

The highlight of the night was the cutting of the spectacular anniversary cake, decorated with the club's colors and emblem. This symbolic moment represented the passage of time and the sweet victories achieved during these 50 years of sporting history, as all those present applauded enthusiastically.

Luke giving a speech: Sharing heartfelt words during the event.

Words That Inspire

Luke captivated the audience with an emotional speech that journeyed through the club's history, highlighting the most significant moments and the people who have left their mark. His message of unity and perseverance resonated among attendees, inspiring both veterans and the new generations of athletes who are part of the LVAC family.

During our 50th anniversary celebration, this video was screened showcasing the rich history of our club from its beginnings to the present day. Relive the most memorable moments and meet the people who have made LVAC what it is today.
