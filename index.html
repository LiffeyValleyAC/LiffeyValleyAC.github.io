Tom Brennan 5k New Year's Day Race 2025

Congratulations to our 2025 Winners Laura Mooney (Tullamore Harriers AC) and Tadgh Donnelly (Drogheda & District A) who won in times of 16.48 and 14.38 respectively

Full Results and Race Report at MyRunResults

We are very proud to host this annual 5k race which celebrates the achievement of our legendary club mate Tom Brennan's 1975 National Cross Country Championship Triumph, which took place in the inaugural year of the club.

Thank you to all participants of this year's race, including our fellow Athletics Ireland Clubs and those athletes kicking off the Lord Mayor's 5 Alive Challenge 2025 which is now in its impressive 13th year
